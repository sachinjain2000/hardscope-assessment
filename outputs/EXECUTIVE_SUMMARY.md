## The Big Picture

We tracked six major VALORANT creators across 112 videos using the YouTube Data API v3 to answer one question: **Where should we put our money in Q1 2025?**

One creator (tarik) is actually growing against the market trend, while others are becoming too expensive for the engagement they provide.

## Strategic Creator Insights

### The Counter Cyclical Win: tarik
Tarik is our strongest signal. While the entire VALORANT category search interest dropped from 65.4 to 36.7 (a 44% dip), tarik actually grew his engagement rate by 1.14 percentage points. This shows a "hardscope" audience that stays loyal regardless of game hype cycles. He is our top recommendation for a budget scale up.

### The Normalization: TenZ
TenZ remains our reach leader. His Q3 numbers were skewed by a viral 7.4M view Short, but his Q4 performance of 2.93% ER is actually very healthy when normalized. He provides the massive awareness floor we need for any major activation.

### The Structural Issue: aceu
Aceu is currently our most expensive creator. With a CPE of $2.42 (14 times the efficient ceiling) and a declining engagement trend (2.79% ER), he is not delivering the ROI we need at his current estimated rate. This is likely an audience fit issue rather than a content quality issue.
